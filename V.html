About

Hello, I'm Rahul

I graduated in Mathematics, but my heart has always been drawn toward technology. I started my career as a computer operator, where hands-on experience became my greatest teacher — deepening both my skills and my curiosity for the digital world.

Life doesn't always follow the path we plan. Family responsibilities meant formal tech courses were out of reach, so I channelled my energy into supporting the people I love — and into finding beauty in the world around me.

Photography became that outlet. Armed with nothing but my smartphone, I wander into nature and try to capture moments that words can't quite hold — moonlit clouds, dewy leaves, bare branches against a glowing sun, the stillness of a twilight pond. I'm not a professional; I'm just someone who sees.
